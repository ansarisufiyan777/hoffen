Drilling Rig manning for Offshore and Onshore Rigs

We provide drilling companies with conventional and specialist crews for drilling jobs. We offer services from the start of a project right to the end, giving our clients a comprehensive one-stop solution for all their personnel needs. We actively market both well-drilling and program engineering services and provide total drilling operations management for offshore jack-up and land drilling.

Onshore and offshore catering services

Working with your existing on-site kitchen and mess, we provide high-quality onshore and offshore catering services for your crew that are hygienic, safe and nutritious. If required, we can even build kitchen and mess facilities from scratch.

Oilfield safety and technical training

We facilitate training courses for conventional jobs such as drillers and crane operators, as well as specialist training courses such as fire and safety courses, HUETS, sea survival courses and rig evacuation courses.

Offshore warehouse and logistics services

We have the facilities to store our Client’s equipment for short or long-term leases so that rig has the sufficient space for crew to work safely and efficiently if there is a delay in Client’s operations or a sudden change of plans.

Offshore accommodation and medical services

We have partnerships and relationships with leading credible partners to provide accommodation and medical services. For accommodation, this includes short and long-term stays in corporate hotels or service apartments. Medical services include executive health screenings, pre-employment check-ups, reconstructive surgery and other medical services.

Supplies and services for offshore emergencies

Sometimes emergency issues appear that fall outside Client’s usual core operations and functions. Often these require specific supplies or custom services that only a local domestic specialist can help with. Hoffen Group can provide these supplies and services, and aim to find the appropriate solutions for any emergency during the operations with 24X7 backend team support.

Repair of Rig Equipments as per API & CAT IV Guidelines

Hoffen Group primary focus is to drive growth by offering customers a unique “one-stop” service experience for their oilfield drilling equipment needs. The global demand for equipment in the oil & gas exploration sector has been in a consistent growth pattern for several years, and Hoffen Group’s ability to offer field service, capital equipment and parts sales, on board / on site equipments repair as per API and CAT IV guidelines, in house shop for fabrication, and abrasive blast and coating has spurred growth and driven the company to expand its capabilities over the years.
